Either party may terminate this agreement upon written notice of thirty (30) days. The terminating party must provide notice in writing to the other party's designated representative. Termination shall be effective upon expiration of the notice period.

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the state of California, without regard to its conflict of law principles. All disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration.

4. RENEWAL TERMS

Unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days prior to the expiration date, this agreement shall automatically renew for additional periods of one (1) year on the same terms and conditions.

Payment shall be made within 30 days of invoice receipt. Invoices must be submitted in accordance with the fee schedule agreed upon in writing by both parties. Failure to pay may result in suspension of services.
